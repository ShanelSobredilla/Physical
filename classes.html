OUR CLASSES

Yoga helps strengthen the cardiopulmonary function. We provide a wide variety of yoga classes for different students. You can choose any classes that are most suitable for your level and ability.
BOLLYWOOD DANCE
"Bollywood dance” is all the rage! The inspiration of “Bollywood dance” is from the Indian movie, the dance fused the classical Indian dance “Bhangra”, the modern street corner dance "Hip Hop” and other dances.
AEROBICS
Combining the rhythmic steps of aerobics with graceful dance movements, Aerobics has blossomed into a sport for all to have fun while losing weight and staying in shape.

KICK BOXING
Thai-Boxing exercise is beloved by most females because of the great variation and challenging. It enhances health and body and trains up the reaction, agility and coordination, which fulfill the females’ dreams of fat burning and body shaping.
BELLY DANCE (A)
